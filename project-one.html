My Portfolio Website

as a Freelancer

This is a mini-project as part of theKodeGoBootcamp that I attented for four months (September-December 2022). I decided to make this project strategically as this project is done individually and it served as my guide and inspiration to finished the said bootcamp. With my previous knowledge in UX design, I implemented styles that I see fit for this project. Furthermore, the skills taught to us in the Bootcamp in front-end dev made this possible. Lastly, with some exposure to journalism and graphics design, I created most of the content in this project.

Project Summary

This portfolio website showcases my works in web development, graphics design, UX design, and blogging. Furthermore this contains some of my backgrounds, what I value and my social links. As this website contains four-responsive pages with relevant contents for my prospects.

The Problem

As the freelancing industry gets saturated, freelancers struggle to find prosepect clients on fdifferent gig platform, such as fiver,freelancer,upwork, and others. With this challenges I felt imcompetent due to no replies from sending proprosals, which might be to no proof of work.

Business Problem

Becuase I am struggling to gain prospects, this has lead to:

  1. 1.) Imposter syndrome that put me at risk with my mental health
  2. 2.) Opportunity cost of sending countless emails and proposals

User Goal:

I would like my prospects to be informed of what services I offers, my workflows, my pricing and some of my works.

Business Goal:

To filter out people who are actually interested to with with me and is capable of providing great value.

Design Principle

Development Process

> Technologies used are HTML/CSS, Bootstrap Framework, GitHub and GitHub pages

> Started from top level layouting down to individual elements of the website.

> Content created are only necessay graphics and contexts.

> Plugging in the content and organizing the paddings, alignments and repsonsiveness.

Result:

A red, 2D/3D, and minimal themed website showcasing my works, blogs and some of my backgrounds.
